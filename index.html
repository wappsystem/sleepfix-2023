Welcome to the SleepFix Study

SleepFix is a smartphone application designed to treat insomnia using Sleep Restriction Therapy (SRT) which works to improve the balance between actual sleep time and the total amount of time spent in bed including time spent awake and asleep.

You are invited to take part in this online research study using SleepFix that aims to improve your sleep and daytime functioning.

You may be eligible to participate if you:

Are above 18 years old

Have access to a smartphone

Have insomnia
